Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide a thrilling ride to an experienced white water rafter and to provide an affordible ride to the newcomers who have never experienced white water rafting. we can achieve this by keeping the costs low for the people with little to no experice and guiding them down a simple river while providing a high quality trip to those who have done it berfore. we will keep cost low for the begginer experice and take them down very few tame rapids and as they gane more experience we will take them on longer and more intese trips.

Audience

Our audiance are people who like to be outside and want to rafing. we need to keep in mind the new comers who have never gone before so they don't know anything abour White Water Rafting, our font/colors/images should be simple but still portray the experience of rafting. the images should show some rapids that while big are not intense enough to scare people away. a lot of our demographic is aimed to be newcomers no matter the age especially those in their 20s-30s who are looking for a fun experiece with either their family or friends.
