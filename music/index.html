21st Century Schizoid Man by King Crimson

21st Century Schiziod Man is a great Driving song to walk to. It's a longer song with very little lyrics. But it makes up for the great Jazz-Rock instrumentals in the song. The tempo and the intenseity of the song leaves nothing to be desired. I must admit when I first heard the song I was not a fan. I did not like it at all. But it has definetley grown on me as time has passed on.

Kickstart my Heart by Mötley Crüe

Nothing screams speeding tickets than roaring electic guitars and some sick drums. Thankfully Mötley Crüe has created arguably one of the greatest songs to speed to. Don't take my advice but there are some songs that you can just feel the extra horsepower in your engine and Kickstart my does this perfectly.
